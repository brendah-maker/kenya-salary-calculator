Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about how the calculator works.

1. What does this calculator do?

It calculates your Net Salary after statutory deductions including PAYE, NSSF, and SHIF using updated 2026 Kenya rates.

2. Can I calculate gross salary from net salary?

Yes. On the home page, switch the calculation mode to “Gross Salary (from Net)” to reverse-calculate your gross income.

3. Are the calculations accurate?

The calculator follows Kenya’s 2026 PAYE bands, NSSF structure, and SHIF contribution rules. However, always confirm with official sources for tax planning or legal purposes.

4. Is this tool free?

Yes. The platform is free to use and supported through advertisements to keep it accessible to everyone.
