About Kenya Financial Tools Hub

Kenya Financial Tools Hub was created to help employees, freelancers, and employers easily calculate their net salary after statutory deductions such as PAYE, NSSF, SHIF, and VAT.

Many people do not clearly understand how much they take home after deductions. This platform simplifies complex tax calculations into clear, easy-to-read breakdowns.

What This Platform Offers

  • Accurate PAYE calculations based on Kenya tax bands
  • NSSF Tier I and Tier II breakdown
  • SHIF contribution calculator (2.75% of gross salary)
  • VAT calculator
  • Salary comparison insights

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote financial awareness and transparency by giving Kenyans simple tools to understand income, deductions, and tax obligations.

Future updates will include budgeting tools, financial planning features, and additional calculators tailored for the Kenyan market.
