About This Platform

Kenya Financial Tools Hub was created to help employees, freelancers, and employers easily calculate their net salary after statutory deductions such as PAYE, NSSF, and SHIF using updated 2026 rates.

Many people do not fully understand how much they take home after taxes. This platform simplifies complex calculations into clear, easy-to-read breakdowns.

What This Tool Covers

  • PAYE calculation based on Kenya tax bands
  • NSSF Tier I and Tier II contributions
  • SHIF contributions (2.75% of gross salary)
  • VAT calculations
  • Salary comparison insights

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote financial awareness and transparency by giving Kenyans simple tools to understand their income and statutory deductions.

Future updates will include budgeting tools, tax insights, and additional financial calculators tailored for the Kenyan market.
