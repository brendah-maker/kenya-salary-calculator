About Kenya Payroll Deductions

Kenyan employees have several statutory deductions from their salary, including PAYE, NSSF, SHIF, and Housing Levy. Understanding these helps you plan your finances effectively.

Kenya PAYE Tax Bands 2026

First KES 24,000 – 10%

KES 24,001 – 32,333 – 25%

KES 32,334 – 47,667 – 30%

KES 47,668 – 100,000 – 32.5%

Above KES 100,000 – 35%

Personal Relief: KES 2,400 per month

SHIF Key Details

  • Mandatory for all Kenyan residents (employed and self-employed)
  • Minimum contribution: KES 300 per month
  • Tax Relief: 15% tax relief on SHIF contribution
  • Employers must remit deductions by the 9th of the following month
