How PAYE is Calculated in Kenya (2026)

PAYE (Pay As You Earn) is income tax deducted from employees' salaries based on progressive tax bands set by the Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA). The higher your income, the higher the tax band applied.

Key Salary Deductions in 2026

  • PAYE Tax: Calculated using official KRA income tax bands.
  • NSSF: Mandatory pension contribution for retirement savings.
  • SHIF: Social Health Insurance Fund deduction.
  • Housing Levy: Affordable housing contribution percentage.
