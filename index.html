How PAYE is Calculated in Kenya (2026)

PAYE (Pay As You Earn) is income tax deducted from employees' salaries according to Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) tax bands. This calculator uses the latest 2026 PAYE tax rates including:

  • PAYE Tax Bands
  • NSSF Contributions
  • SHIF Deductions
  • Affordable Housing Levy
